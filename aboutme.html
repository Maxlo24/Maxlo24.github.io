Who am I?

MSc student in a double degree program in Image, Modeling & Computing at CPE Lyon and in innovation management at Emlyon business school

I am passionate about new technology, specifically Artificial Intelligence. I am currently pursuing a second degree in Business and Innovation at Emlyon Business School after my fifth year of engineering school at CPE Lyon.
I aspire to work in the field of machine learning and upstream innovation.

I recently returned from a gap year at the University of Michigan, where I worked with an international team as a researcher to develop machine learning-based imaging/quantification tools. Through publishing scientific papers and presenting them at conferences, I have demonstrated my skills in innovation, teamwork, and development.

With a Growth Mindset I know that I can change over time, I'm therefore more open to reflect, learn and grow from challenges. As a dynamic, applied, and responsible individual, I am proactive and enjoy working in teams with a strong desire to succeed. I adapt easily to any situation.
